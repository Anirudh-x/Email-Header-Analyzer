Frequently Asked Questions

What is an email header?
An email header contains information about the sender, recipient, subject, and other metadata related to an email message.
How do I find email headers?
Email headers can usually be found in the settings or options menu of your email client. Look for an option to view the full message headers.
What is an email header analyzer?
An email header analyzer is a tool or service that allows users to inspect the header section of an email to gather information about its origin, path, and delivery.
Why would I need to analyze email headers?
Analyzing email headers can help users identify the source of an email, verify its authenticity, diagnose delivery issues, detect spam or phishing attempts, and understand the routing path of the email.
What information can I obtain from email headers?
Email headers typically contain details such as sender and recipient addresses, server IP addresses, message routing information, timestamps, and message ID. Analyzing these elements can provide insights into the email's journey from sender to recipient.
